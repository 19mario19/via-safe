Careers at ViaSafe

ViaSafe is not just a technology project; it's a movement shaping the future of mobility on the planet. We are looking for talented, ambitious, and passionate professionals ready to join our team and contribute to creating a safer, more efficient, and inclusive world.

Vacancies

We are constantly looking for specialists in various fields: from AI developers, data engineers, and cybersecurity experts to urban logistics specialists, transportation system analysts, and partnership development managers.

Keep an eye on current vacancies on our website in the 'Careers' section. We offer competitive salaries, opportunities for professional growth, work on global-scale projects, and comfortable working conditions.

Company Philosophy

  • Innovation in DNA: We believe in bold ideas and continuous experimentation. Our team is at the forefront of technological progress, creating solutions that change the world.
  • Collaboration and Trust: We value open communication, mutual respect, and teamwork. We build long-term relationships with employees, partners, and the community, based on trust and shared goals.
  • Responsibility: We recognize the scale of our impact and are responsible for creating a system that benefits society and the environment. Safety, inclusivity, and sustainability are not just values; they are principles guiding every decision we make.
  • Development and Growth: We support the pursuit of learning and professional development. At ViaSafe, you will find opportunities to realize your potential and continuously improve your skills.
  • People First: Our employees are our greatest asset. We create a stimulating and supportive work environment where everyone can feel part of a large and important endeavor.
